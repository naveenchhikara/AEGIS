# AEGIS Demo Script - 15 Minutes

**Audience:** CEO/MD, Board Members, Senior Management
**Objective:** Show the value AEGIS brings to UCB audit & compliance management
**Demo URL:** http://13.127.219.124

---

## Before You Start

- [ ] Open Chrome/Edge (latest version)
- [ ] Navigate to http://13.127.219.124
- [ ] Have a mobile phone ready for responsive demo
- [ ] Close unnecessary tabs and notifications

---

## 1. Login (1 min)

**URL:** http://13.127.219.124/login

**What to show:**

- AEGIS branding with bank logo
- Professional login form with email, password, and MFA fields
- Security indicators: 256-bit encryption, RBI Compliant, SOC 2 Certified

**What to say:**

> "AEGIS is a secure, cloud-hosted platform designed specifically for Urban Cooperative Banks. It runs on AWS Mumbai servers ensuring your data stays in India, fully compliant with RBI data localization requirements."

**Action:** Enter any email (e.g., `rajesh.deshmukh@apexbank.example`) and any password. Click **Sign In**.

---

## 2. Dashboard Overview (3 min)

**URL:** http://13.127.219.124/dashboard

**What to show:**

- Compliance Health Score (radial chart with percentage)
- Audit Coverage donut chart (completed vs planned)
- Findings count cards (Total, Critical, Open, Overdue)
- Risk Overview panel with contributing factors
- Regulatory Calendar with upcoming deadlines

**What to say:**

> "This is what your CEO sees every morning. At a single glance, you know your compliance health, audit progress, and risk exposure. No more waiting for monthly reports - this is real-time."

**Key talking points:**

- Point to Health Score: "Your overall compliance stands at X%. The board can see this instantly."
- Point to Risk Overview: "AEGIS automatically calculates risk level based on unresolved findings, non-compliant requirements, and overdue items."
- Point to Regulatory Calendar: "Upcoming RBI deadlines - so nothing falls through the cracks."

---

## 3. Compliance Registry (3 min)

**URL:** http://13.127.219.124/compliance

**What to show:**

- Summary cards: Total, Compliant, Partial, Non-Compliant, Pending
- Filterable table with 55 RBI compliance requirements
- Status badges (green/yellow/red/gray)
- Category filter dropdown
- Compliance health trend chart

**What to say:**

> "Every RBI requirement your bank must comply with - in one place. Filter by category, track status, see who's responsible. This is your single source of truth for compliance."

**Action:**

1. Point out the summary cards - "7 compliant, 3 partial, 3 non-compliant"
2. Click category filter - select "Risk Management"
3. Show the trend chart - "Six month compliance trend at a glance"

**Key talking point:**

> "During an RBI inspection, you can pull up any requirement instantly - with status, assigned owner, and evidence trail."

---

## 4. Audit Planning (2 min)

**URL:** http://13.127.219.124/audit-plans

**What to show:**

- Summary cards: Total Audits, Completed, In Progress, Planned
- Calendar view showing FY 2025-26 audit schedule
- Engagement cards with progress bars

**What to say:**

> "Your annual audit plan - always up to date. See which audits are complete, in progress, or upcoming. Each audit tracks team assignments, progress, and findings."

**Action:**

1. Show the calendar view - "April to March, Indian financial year"
2. Switch to card view using the toggle
3. Click on an audit card to open the detail sheet
4. Show progress bar, team members, and findings summary

---

## 5. Audit Findings (3 min)

**URL:** http://13.127.219.124/findings

**What to show:**

- Severity distribution cards (Critical, High, Medium, Low)
- Findings table with sorting and filtering
- Finding detail page with full lifecycle

**What to say:**

> "Track every audit finding from observation to closure. Each finding has a severity, status, root cause analysis, auditee response, and action plan - exactly what RBI expects."

**Action:**

1. Point to severity cards - "1 critical, 3 high severity findings need attention"
2. Click severity filter - select "Critical"
3. Click on a finding row to open the detail page
4. Show: Observation, Root Cause, Risk Impact, Action Plan, Timeline

**Key talking point:**

> "The complete audit trail - from when the finding was raised, to auditee response, to closure. This is what RBI inspectors want to see."
